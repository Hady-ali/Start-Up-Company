Top Tourist Attractions in Cairo, Egypt

Cairo, the capital of Egypt, is rich in history and culture. Some of the top tourist attractions include the Great Pyramids of Giza, the Egyptian Museum, the Citadel of Saladin, Khan El Khalili Bazaar, and the Nile River cruises. These landmarks offer visitors a unique glimpse into Egypt's ancient civilization and vibrant daily life.
